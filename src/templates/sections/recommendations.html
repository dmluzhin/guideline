The Steps Included in the Pre-Work Inspection
Interior Exterior
Checking the seats and carpet for rips, tears or stains.
Checking the condition of front and rear bumpers.
Checking the dashboard and trim for cracks, scratches and damage.
Checking the condition of the vehicle light lenses.
Checking that all equipment controls and switches are present.
Checking the condition of the door mirrors and glass.
Checking that all floor mats are present, and checking for stains and damage.
Checking the body panels for scratches, dents and damage.
Documenting any valuables left inside the vehicle on the pre-work inspection sheet.
Checking the alloy wheels and wheel trims for scratches and cracks.
Checking the windscreen and the other glazing for chips and cracks.
